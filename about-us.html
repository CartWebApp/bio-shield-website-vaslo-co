Our story begins when a group consisting of our CEO, advertisers and scientists came togtherwith one goal in mind:to revolutionize a new bugspray that is:

  • Environmentally friendly
  • User friendly
  • And importantly, renewable!

To begin our research, we partnered with ecolife, a company that spends most of their time in the Amazonian rainforests, where they study and continue to discover how specific plant-life affects specfic animals.


After 5 years of researching, we had discovered the perfect bugspray for our company. And thus came Bioshield, which started small in one town known to have bug issues:Dallas-Fort Worth.
