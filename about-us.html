Our History

Where It Begins

Our story begins when a group consisting of our CEO, advertisers and scientists came together with one goal in mind: to revolutionize a new bug spray that is environment-friendly, user-friendly, and renewable.

To begin our research, we partnered with Ecolife, a company that studies plant life in the Amazon rainforest and how it affects specific animals.

Small Steps

After 7.5 years of research we discovered a formulation that met our goals and launched BioShield in the Dallas–Fort Worth area.

The Expansion

After success in Chicago, we expanded to cities such as:

  • Los Angeles
  • Philadelphia
  • Chicago

Going Global

We now operate internationally in countries including:

  • Spain
  • The UK
  • France
  • Australia
  • Japan

Where We Are Now

We are a global company committed to removing bug problems at home and outdoors. Thank you for reading our history — happy trails!
